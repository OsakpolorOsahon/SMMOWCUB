About SMMOWCUB

Our Mission

SMMOWCUB ("Senior Members of the Man O' War Club, University of Benin") is a brotherhood of alumni who once served with distinction in UNIBEN's storied Man O' War Club. Founded on principles of discipline, service, and leadership, our mission is to keep those bonds alive‚Äîfostering mentorship, professional growth, and community impact long after graduation.

We are an exclusive alumni network open only to graduates who served in the Man O' War Club at UNIBEN. The website has two distinct audiences: Public Visitors and Statesmen (Members).

After Secretary approval, members gain access to our secure members-only section: Empowerment Hub, Job Board, Mentorship Program, Discussion Forums, Interactive Map, Badge Recognition, and Hall of Fame.
